Online Help

Many of the features have tool-tips, which will appear when you hover over the option with the mouse. There are full descriptions of all of the features in the Help Browser. You can access the Help Browser by going to the Help menu or clicking on a question mark button.

Getting Started

Select the type of game you would like to play from the main lobby. Ring Games plays like a cash game against our bots. Tournaments allows you to play a tournament against the bots, either a single table or multiple table against thousands of bots. Poker Academy Online lets you connect our servers over the Internet and play other Poker Academy users online (requires Internet connection).
