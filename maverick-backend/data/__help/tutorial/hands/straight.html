Straight

A Straight is a hand where all five cards are in a sequence according to their ranks. The ranking of straights depends on the highest ranked card in the five-card sequence making up the straight. As with straight flushes, Aces can serve as the highest ranked card in an Ace-high straight, or as the lowest ranked card in a Five-high straight.

The best possible straight is an Ace-high straight (e.g. As Ks Qd Jc Th), and the worst possible straight is a Five-high straight (e.g. 5s 4s 3d 2c Ah).
