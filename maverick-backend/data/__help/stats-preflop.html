Pre-Flop Stats

The pre-flop stats table breaks your hands down by pre-flop starting hand, and shows how much you have won or lost with each type of starting hand. This is very useful for learning which hands you should either fold more often, or learn to play differently post-flop. By default, hands you are winning with are shown in green, hands you are break-even with are shown in white, and hands you are losing with are shown in red.

Note: Don't read too much into your hand values until you have collected enough hands to have statistical significance. You will need hundreds of examples for each starting hand before the values are meaningful.

Tip: Double click a row to bring up a Hand History Browser showing all of the hand histories where you had the selected hole cards. For instance, if you notice you're losing money with a particular hand type, you can instantly bring up all of the relevant hand histories for review.
