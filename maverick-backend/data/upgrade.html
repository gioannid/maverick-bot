Poker Academy Pro Features

Enhanced AI
This version includes two additional advanced Artificial Intelligence bots. Vexbot is a heads-up specialist bot designed specifically to find weaknesses in your game. Sparbot is a game theory optimal play bot that will always try to make the best decisions based on the current game state.

Hand Evaluator
Enter any hand and instantly see what draws it can make, what the nuts for the board are, what hands your opponent could have, and much more.

Advanced Player Statistics
Additional information is available in the player statistics module. This information provides more in depth details on your play. This includes who's winning against you, who you are losing to, which starting hands you are having the most success with and how lucky you are getting while playing. Filter stats by table size, position, or starting hands to see how your earnings change in each scenario.

Luckometer Analysis
Ever wonder if your winning streak was a result of good play or just lucky cards, or if a losing streak was just a bad run or genuine tilt? Our luckometer can plot your luck against your winnings and reveal trends that resulted from good or bad luck.

Hand History Playback
